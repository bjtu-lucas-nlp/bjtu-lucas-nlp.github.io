About Me

Bridging AI Theory with Real-World Applications

I am a PhD candidate at the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney, working under the supervision of Distinguished Professor Jie Lu and Professor Guangquan Zhang.

My research focuses on developing interpretable and trustworthy cross-domain recommendation algorithms, with particular emphasis on leveraging Large Language Models to enhance personalization and user experience.

I received my M.S. degree in Computer Technology from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2020, where I developed a passion for machine learning and its applications in healthcare and recommender systems.

Research Interests

Exploring the Frontiers of AI and Machine Learning

Large Language Models

Developing advanced LLM applications for personalized recommendations and natural language understanding in user preferences.

Cross-Domain Recommendations

Creating algorithms that transfer knowledge across domains to solve data sparsity and cold-start problems.

Deep Learning

Advancing neural network architectures for interpretable and trustworthy recommendation systems.

AI in Healthcare

Applying machine learning to genomics-enhanced cancer risk prediction and personalized healthcare systems.
